About Benin

Porto-Novo, the capital city of Benin

The Republic of Benin

Benin, officially the Republic of Benin, is a country in West Africa. It is bordered by Togo to the west, Nigeria to the east, Burkina Faso to the north-west, and Niger to the north-east. The majority of its population lives on the southern coastline of the Bight of Benin, part of the Gulf of Guinea in the northernmost tropical portion of the Atlantic Ocean.

Youth Context

Benin has a predominantly young population, with over 65% under the age of 25. This creates both challenges and opportunities for youth development, leadership, and employment - making AIESEC's mission particularly relevant and impactful in the Beninese context.
