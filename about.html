About Benin

The Heart of West Africa

Benin is a culturally rich and historically significant country in West Africa, often referred to as the cradle of the Vodun culture, which influences traditions far beyond its borders.

Vodun Days Celebration

Every year, Vodun Days (January 10th) bring together thousands of people from around the world in Ouidah to celebrate spirituality, heritage, and identity.

Heritage & Modernity

With iconic sites such as the Royal Palaces of Abomey (UNESCO World Heritage), the historic Door of No Return, and the modern transformation of cities like Cotonou.

Our Growth

Original Structure

Local Committees

  • AIESEC ENEAM - First local committee of AIESEC in Benin

  • AIESEC ENS Porto-Novo - Third local committee
