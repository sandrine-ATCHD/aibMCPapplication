MCP Role & Eligibility

Eligibility

Must be between 18 and 30 years old. This ensures alignment with AIESEC's youth leadership mandate.

Age Requirement

Lead

Ensure external relevance and maintain strategic coherence. Uniting and establishing a shared direction for all members.

Strategic Leadership

Manage

Develop strategic direction, act as the guardian of legal processes, and uphold accountability within the team.

Operational Excellence

Represent

Responsible for AIESEC in Malawi's positioning in the Global Plenary and representing the entity to all external stakeholders.

Global Ambassadorship

The MCP role integrates these four key dimensions to drive AIESEC Malawi's success and impact across national and global platforms.
