Job Description: Overall MCP Responsibilities

Overview

The MCP is responsible for leading and managing AIESEC in Benin, providing strategic direction for the organization to grow sustainably. This includes representing AIESEC in Benin to both internal and external stakeholders, organizing planning processes, implementing tracking and decision-making systems, coaching MCVPs on the strategic plan, supporting budgeting and financial decisions, and ensuring proper HR and MC processes are followed.

Lead

  • Guardian of the AIESEC Way (Vision and Values).
  • Clarity of your purpose (Selfish & Selfless).
  • Define your non-negotiable must-wins and dreams.
  • Be conscious of your leadership style.
  • Team selection based on purpose, actions, and orientation.
  • Strength-based leadership, enabling team members to complement one another.
  • Develop the best leadership pipeline to ensure legacy.
  • Measure of Success: 100% entity performance growth & 100% MC members performing.

Manage

  • Guardian of the legal representation.
  • Co-responsible for financial long-term sustainability while ensuring growth-assured investments.
  • Ensure accountability within team and plenary.
  • Responsible as President of the plenary and as Leader of the region.

Represent

  • Manage external stakeholders: SG/BOD, Partners, Government, Enablers, Media, Events, & R&Rs.
  • Ensure fulfillment of Global Membership Criteria.
  • Represent AIESEC in Benin at all official touchpoints (International, National, Local Conferences, Summits, ILMS).

Profile: Applicant Criteria

Eligibility Criteria

  • Strong knowledge about AIESEC
  • Had MC, EB, or LCP experience
  • Active member of the organization for at least two years
  • Agree to commit to completing your mandate
  • Participation in international AIESEC conferences (TLC, IC, MEAXLDS, etc.)
  • Commitment to end term once elected
  • Pay application fees (10.000 FCFA) if application meets all criteria related to the Recruitment Firms and Electoral committee; to AIESEC in BENIN Account before Interview only if your application meet all criteria and have been accept.
