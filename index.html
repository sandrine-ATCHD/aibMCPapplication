Foreword: To the One Who Dares to Lead

Dear Applicant,

With a heart full of enthusiasm and conviction, I leave you these words. Congratulations for taking the step to click on this link. That single action already says something about you: courage, curiosity, and a desire to make a difference.

Before you go further, I want to share a few words out of respect for this entity that shaped my leadership and gave me far more than I could ever give back.

After six years here, I’ve witnessed many forms of leadership each shaping the AIESEC we know today.

Being MCP means accepting the unknown, being misunderstood, and persisting even when reality seems to move against you. It means living through moments where nothing seems to work, yet time keeps moving forward.

Never forget what brought you here.

This role is exactly what you need not just for the entity, but for yourself. It will challenge, reshape, and transform you in ways you cannot yet imagine.

The legacy I received didn’t start with me. It’s the result of sacrifices from over twenty generations before us. Today, that flame is ready to be passed on to the generation that will define the future of this entity.

In moments of exhaustion or doubt, remember: you stand on years of sacrifice, commitment, and belief. Give your best, and trust that this organization will give you its best in return.

My advice: don’t apply for the title or to prove anything. Apply to restore the pride, strength, and credibility of this entity.

It is waiting for you.

I came, I lived the experience, and I grew beyond measure. May this also be your closing line.

Good luck, and see you on the other side.

Armel AHOUADI

Member Committee President, AIESEC in Benin

Job Description:
Overall MCP Responsibilities

Overall

The MCP leads and represents AIESEC in Benin, ensuring strategic direction, organizational growth, and alignment across the entity. The role includes overseeing planning, performance tracking, financial decisions, team management, and partnerships, while safeguarding AIESEC's purpose, values, and overall organizational welfare.

Lead

  • • Uphold and embody the AIESEC vision and values
  • • Define a clear personal and collective purpose with key priorities
  • • Develop conscious, strength-based leadership and high-performing teams
  • • Success: Drive 100% entity performance growth

Manage

  • • Guardian of the legal representation
  • • Co-responsible for financial long-term sustainability
  • • Ensure accountability within team and plenary
  • • Responsible as President of the plenary and as Leader of the region

Represent

  • • Manage external stakeholders: SG/BOA, Partners, Government
  • • Ensure fulfillment of Global Membership Criteria
  • • Represent AIESEC in Benin at all official touchpoints

Profile: Applicant Criteria

Eligibility Criteria

  • Strong knowledge about AIESEC
  • Had MCEB, or LCP experience
  • Active member of the organization for at least two years
  • Have at least participated in an international conference of AIESEC, such as MEAS, XPROs, MEAXLDS, or any other conference outside Benin
  • Commitment to end the term once elected
  • Pay the application fee (10,000 FCFA) related to the Recruitment Firm and the Electoral Committee before the interview, only if your application meets all criteria and is accepted.
  • Has already completed an international exchange with AIESEC or has the ‘Approved’ status on EXPA and has paid ‘Project Fees’ to be ‘Realised’.
