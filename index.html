Job Description: Overall MCP Responsibilities

Overall

The MCP leads and represents AIESEC in Benin, ensuring strategic direction, organizational growth, and alignment across the entity. The role includes overseeing planning, performance tracking, financial decisions, team management, and partnerships, while safeguarding AIESEC's purpose, values, and overall organizational welfare.

Lead

  • • Guardian of the AIESEC Way (Vision and Values)
  • • Clarity of your purpose (Selfish & Selfless)
  • • Define your non-negotiable must-wins and dreams
  • • Be conscious of your leadership style
  • • Team selection based on purpose, actions, and orientation
  • • Strength-based leadership
  • • Develop the best leadership pipeline
  • • Success: 100% entity performance growth

Manage

  • • Guardian of the legal representation
  • • Co-responsible for financial long-term sustainability
  • • Ensure accountability within team and plenary
  • • Responsible as President of the plenary and as Leader of the region

Represent

  • • Manage external stakeholders: SG/BOD, Partners, Government
  • • Ensure fulfillment of Global Membership Criteria
  • • Represent AIESEC in Benin at all official touchpoints

Profile: Applicant Criteria

Eligibility Criteria

  • Strong knowledge about AIESEC
  • Had MC, EB, or LCP experience
  • Active member of the organization for at least two years
  • Agree to commit to completing your mandate
  • Have at least participated in international conferences of AIESEC, such as TLC, IC, MEAXLDS, or any other conference outside Benin
  • Commitment to end term once elected
  • Pay Application Fees (10.000 FCFA), related to the Recruitment Firms and Electoral committee; to AIESEC in BENIN Account before Interview only if your application meet all criteria and have been accept.
