MCP Role & Eligibility

Eligibility

Must be between 18 and 30 years old. This ensures alignment with AIESEC's youth leadership mandate.

Age Requirement

Lead

Ensure external relevance and maintain strategic coherence. Uniting and establishing a shared direction for all members.

Strategic Leadership

Manage

Develop strategic direction, act as the guardian of legal processes, and uphold accountability within the team.

Operational Excellence

Represent

Responsible for AIESEC in Benin's positioning in the Global Plenary and representing the entity to all external stakeholders.

Global Ambassadorship

The MCP role integrates these four key dimensions to drive AIESEC in Benin's success and impact across national and global platforms.

Questionnaire Breakdown

Detailed questions covering all aspects of leadership, management, and representation for AIESEC in Benin.

Personal

  • Explain why you want to serve as MCP.
  • What value will you bring from your first day to your last?
  • Why do you believe you are the right person to lead the entity?

Lead

  • Leadership Style: Identify three traits and explain how your style meets Benin's needs.
  • Relevance & Impact: Why does Benin need AIESEC? How can AIESEC in Benin create meaningful nationwide impact?

Manage

  • Strategies: Propose three main strategies for: Membership, Exchange, Finance, and Brand (base on SWOT/PESTEL).
  • Culture: Describe current culture, desired changes, and your plan for growth and results.
  • Financial Sustainability: Assess current situation and strategies to strengthen long-term stability.
  • Vision Toward A2030: Contribution to A2025 and your vision for A2030 contribution.

Represent

  • External Relations: Assess current relations and how you will position AIESEC as a relevant, trusted organization.
  • Key Trends Shaping Benin: Identify three trends and how AIESEC should stand in relation to them.
  • Global Representation: Evaluate current global presence and plan for strengthening international touchpoints.
  • Board Management: Assess current board management, and improvements for an effective board for 26.27.
