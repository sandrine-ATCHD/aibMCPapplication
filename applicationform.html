MCP 26.27 APPLICATION

Application Package

Please submit the following documents and materials as part of your MCP application. Ensure all files are in PDF format unless stated otherwise.

Biography (1 PDF Page)

Introduce yourself answering these questions: What is for me the purpose of my life, including your top personal values, greatest strengths and weaknesses that drive your behaviors.

CV (Max 2 Pages)

Include your most important information (Name & Surname, Nationality, Place & Date of Birth, Email, Educational Background, Current Position in AIESEC, AIESEC Experiences, Professional Experiences, Skills, Proficiency in languages...).

Motivation Letter (1 PDF Page)

Addressed to the current MCP. Answer: What is driving you to continue in AIESEC and why are you applying for MCP? What do you want to get out of this experience?

Application Questionnaire & Executive Summary

Application Questionnaire (Max 15 Pages, including front & back covers)

Executive Summary (1 PDF Page)

Video & Blank Paper Challenge

Video: Introduce yourself, your vision, strategies, and why you should be selected as MCP (Max 5 minutes, landscape, professional format)

Blank Paper Challenge: Max 1 Page PDF, be creative.

Endorsement Letters (4)

  • From someone you have led
  • From someone that led you (except current MCP)
  • From a past MCP
  • From an external person

Personal Tests & Action Plan

Merged PDF of results: Enneagram, Prism (Leadership), MBTI, Emotional Intelligence, Team Roles

Recap of the action plan of the current government (1 PDF page)

Application Questions

Who are you? Introduce yourself and your AIESEC experience in a creative way!

What are your 3 key values? How do these shape you as a leader and how are they manifested in your everyday leadership?

Discuss one of your strengths, one of your weaknesses, one of your biggest achievements and a biggest failure and explain how these have shaped you as a person and will they influence your role as MCP?

What is driving you to stay in AIESEC? How is it supporting you to reach your personal goals and where you want to see yourself in 3 years?

List two most important AIESEC experiences and one experience outside of AIESEC with key achievements, key learnings and failures (both quantitatively and qualitatively). How will each contribute to your role as MCP?

Provide an analysis of the external environment of the country. What trends can AIESEC in Benin capitalize on to grow and develop in 25.26? Which program or service can benefit the most and how?

How is AIESEC 2030 shaping the direction of the organization? What are the implications that you foresee for the upcoming term and how do you plan on working with these?

Considering the past 5 years of AIESEC in Benin, make an analysis and highlight the key successes and learnings! What are the trends and evolutions that shaped the organization that we know today, and how will you utilize them in your term as MCP?

Assess the current culture & behaviors membership of AIESEC in Benin. What are the positive and negative points and explain the behaviours/culture you want to shape in the next term in order for us to achieve sustainable growth?

What does leadership mean to you? What is the needed leadership style for the next year and how will this be reflected by you?

What bothers you most about the organization today? As MCP 25.26 how would you start changing these things and how would you engage your MC team and your plenary with it?

What is the identity and culture AIESEC in Benin needs for the term 25.26? How will your profile and leadership shape this?

What is your vision for AIESEC in Benin for the term 25.26?

Based on the current state of the Entity and on your vision and strategic direction, how would your MC structure and supporting bodies look like? How would it be reflected at local level?

Based on your analysis of our LCs and on your understanding of our Plenary, what are the key pillars you would follow in order to ensure proper support for our LCs?

Create a detailed plan for the first three months of the mandate (Actions, objectives, timeline, stakeholders).

What should be the role of the different layers of the organization (MC, EB, MM, Member)? What needs to happen next year for that role to become a reality?
